1. Design Philosophy

Visual principles that make Broad Listener feel trustworthy, modern, and useful for government teams and the public alike.

Core Principles
  • 🏛️
    Institutional trust
    Colours, type, and layout convey the seriousness of public decision-making. Clarity and evidence over gimmicks.
  • 🌊
    Calm confidence
    Generous whitespace, subtle shadows, muted backgrounds—even when handling emotionally charged public input.
  • 🔍
    Evidence-first
    Every visual pattern emphasises traceability. Quotes link to sources. Summaries link to evidence. Clusters link to statements.
  • 👥
    Approachable for participants
    Public-facing surfaces use warm accents and inviting language. The interface feels like a conversation, not a government form.
Why These Choices

Broad Listener serves two very different audiences: government administrators who need data density, audit trails, and defensible outputs—and members of the public who need clarity, warmth, and low cognitive load.

The deep blue-teal primary palette signals authority and reliability—essential for procurement conversations and council presentations. The warm amber accent adds approachability to calls-to-action and public participation surfaces.

The typography (Inter) balances humanist warmth with excellent readability at small sizes, ideal for both data-heavy dashboards and long-form public results pages.
