# Code Of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard:

- be respectful
- critique ideas directly, not people
- keep technical disagreements evidence-based
- assume good faith unless there is strong reason not to

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, or sustained bad-faith disruption.

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate this standard.

For serious concerns, contact the maintainer privately before escalating publicly.
